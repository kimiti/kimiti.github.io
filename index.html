    Welcome to GCC

    Grace Community Center is an apostolic church that is committed to the application of the gospel of the Kingdom of God in the lives of the people. It is our deep desire to seek more understanding of the word of God under the guidance of the Holy Spirit because all we know is not all there is to know. God is revealing more truth from His word to equip and prepare the Church for the second coming of Christ. GCC is are part of a Global Network of autonomous Churches referred to as Kingdom Community Network which is one of the sectors of Congress WBN

    People's Groups.

    The people groups represent the point at which the major segments of the community are identified and recognized. Each people group has its own schedule of meetings and community activities. Currently the people groups in the community are as follows:!

    • Hebron Company.

      This is the group for men aged 21 years and above. It is made up of the following small groups:
      - Calebs
      - Gibbor
      - Sons of Issachar.

    • Ladies of Grace.

      This is the group for women from age of 21 and above It is made up of the following small groups:
      - Pioneering Star
      - Zion Eagles
      - Pace setters
      - Salmons.

    • Visionaries.

      This is the group for young people aged between 13-30 years It is made up of the following small groups:
      - Omega Company
      - Covenant Keepers.

    • Generation of Destiny.

      These are young people aged below 13 years.
