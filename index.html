ABOUT

Vanguard Ed is your bridge to a global learning experience in your field.

WHAT

We develop best-practise visits that take you to leading global hubs in your field, designed to enhance your understanding of your area of interest.

HOW

We connect you with companies, start-ups and academia in your hub of interest that expands your understanding of real-life applications in your field

WHY

In a fast changing world, ideas are transforming the way we live. Our passion is to provide you with a platform that expands your perspective to help you develop global solutions in your industry..

DESIGNED LEARNING EXPERIENCES

Our one-two week immersive programs aim to bridge the gap between education and industry. We help you transport your subject matter from the classroom and into the world and combine study and culture to provide a valuable all round learning experience.
